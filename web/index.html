About Us

RAMKRISHNA CHARITABLE FOUNDATION, popularly known as SABAR PAASHE SABAR SAATHE is a registered non-profit organization in Kolkata that has been working for the cause of Food and Education for all since 2019 and striving to make this world a better place for them in all ways possible. This non profitable organization is the brainchild of Late Ramprasad Dutta (Tutu Da) whose undying compassion for the poor and the needy is nothing short of an inspiration in today’s time when the poor starve and suffer irrevocably due to hunger and lack of money and inspiration for education.

RKCF, aspires to establish a harmonious co-relation between the poor and rich and bring them closer to one another through its comprehensive programs. Due to its exemplary work since 2019, it has focused mainly on betterment of Poor and the needy ,by providing food, books to promote the basic education for the girls and women, helping the poor and needy to get treatment or medicines,helping natural disasters victims, charity like food and shelter to the needy.

RKCF, has now become a name that is synonymous with the welfare of the poor. Through its efforts and services this organization has managed well being for not only the poor but also to the needy. This organization seeks to send out the message that by looking after the poor and needy we not only help them but help ourselves by uplifting our living conditions, health, economy and the environment that surrounds us!

One of its main goals is to ensure that no children on the streets get deprived from proper education and be malnourished. RKCF invites you to join and hands in this noble venture through active donation or by becoming a volunteer. Every step that we take towards the direction of welfare for the poor and needy can make a difference. It’s time to start now!

From The Director's Desk

Every day, girls face barriers to education caused by poverty, cultural norms and practices, poor infrastructure, violence, and fragility.The foremost factor limiting female education is poverty as a majority of the people are poor and cannot afford to give education to their children.

People sometimes forget about others and selfishly pursue only their own interests.

But helping poor people is one of the best gifts a person can give others. Giving them moral support and respect will make them feel that someone really does care about them.

Building safe and inclusive learning environments for the poor has always been a dream, and if the dream is big, it is not possible to be fulfilled by one person. To make the things more constructive, and to value the dreams of others, the NGO has been formed, so that like minds can be under one umbrella and can work together for the noble cause of Food and Education for all.

Raja Mallick
Director,RKCF

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

The main focus will be to promote the basic education for the girls and women

To assist the underprivileged women by providing proper training, knowledge and equipments in various technical and non technical fields for generation of revenue and employment with no profit motive. The revenue generated in this process will be used to promote education for the children of the underprivileged area.

Health purpose: Like helping poor and needy to get treatment or medicines, helping natural disasters victims, charity like food and shelter to the needy. Help to under privilege children’s by providing treatment and equipments.

Vision

We envision a community where everyone has access to good quality, nutritious food, proper education and understands the consequences of hunger and poor nutrition and education for women and is committed to creating a stronger, healthier Society.

Utpal Das

As a matter of nature people in our country and elsewhere are self-centric for two reasons: a) financial pressures and b) competition in all cross of life style. But this great virus COVID-19 have now pushed the mankind into such extreme that we have all started thinking about our neighborhood and also towards the society.

This pandemic has also pushed me to think around. While that I was in my thought process of doing good for the society during this pandemic, I came across a personality by the name Tutu’da (Shri Ram Prosad Dutta). With his self-less deeds of running ration for the poorest of the poor under the BPL and downwards - all with his lone sources. I was moved with his endeavors of organization and taking everybody together in his noble efforts, whether a friend or a foe.

All this put together I became a part of this movement to serve the society and people in real need. Tutu’da motivated our younger brothers in a such a way that each one had now decided to share a part of their earnings for this noble cause of serving the society at large and ultimately formed this society ‘Ram Krishna Charitable Foundation’. I am sure with such humble background and noble cause in hand this Charitable organization will go a very long way in fulfilling its objectives to serve the mankind.

I vow my alliance to this Foundation and wish them all the very best in all their endeavors.

Munmun Das

Food security remains a distant dream to a large section of people who do not have enough food available to remain healthy nor do they have sufficiently diverse diets that provide adequate levels of micronutrients.

Being in the Food and Catering industry it is to be known that most caterers work with 5 to 10 percent overage, so there is a good chance there will be leftover food, prepared but not served, to help feed the hungry and promoting a post event donation of food is not only good but it also encourages others in the industry to do the same good work.

In a country where 14% of the people are undernourished, it is a pleasure in being associated with this Organisation to create a avenue to donate the leftover food to the needy.
